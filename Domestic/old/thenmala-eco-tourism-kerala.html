Detailed Itinerary

Arrive at destination. Transfer to your hotel. Spend the evening relaxing or exploring the local market. Overnight stay.

After breakfast, proceed to visit India’s first Eco-Tourism destination. Enjoy the scenic beauty and cultural significance of this location. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Breakfast at hotel. Check-out and transfer to the airport/railway station for your onward journey. Tour ends with happy memories.

After breakfast, visit the ancient Jakhu Temple atop the highest peak in Shimla. Afternoon: Drive to Kufri for horse riding, nature walks, and panoramic views. Return to Shimla for a relaxed evening.

Post breakfast, enjoy a scenic drive to Annandale or Summer Hill. Visit the Viceregal Lodge for colonial history. Depart from Shimla with fond memories.
