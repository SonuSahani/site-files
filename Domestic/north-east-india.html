DISCOVER NORTH EAST INDIA'S MYSTICAL DESTINATIONS

Explore the emerald paradise of North East India with its lush green hills, ancient monasteries, vibrant tribal cultures, and pristine wildlife sanctuaries. Experience the raw beauty, diverse festivals, and warm hospitality of this hidden gem.

NATURAL WONDERS AND BIODIVERSITY HOTSPOTS

Step into the world of living root bridges, cascading waterfalls, and one-horned rhinos in the lush landscapes of North East. From the wettest place on Earth to sacred lakes, witness nature's unparalleled creativity.

HILL STATIONS AND SPIRITUAL RETREATS

Escape to the snow-capped Himalayas of North East where monasteries echo with chants and tea gardens roll endlessly. Discover the serenity of Tawang, Gangtok, and Majuli - the world's largest river island.
