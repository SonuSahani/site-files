TRIPURA TOUR PACKAGES

Discover the serene hills and palaces. From the majestic Ujjayanta Palace in Agartala to the water palace of Neermahal, experience Tripura's royal heritage, lush tea gardens, and vibrant tribal culture.

LAKES & PALACES

Boat to the stunning Neermahal on Rudrasagar Lake, explore ancient rock carvings at Unakoti, and visit the sacred Tripura Sundari Temple.

Trek through Jampui Hills, spot wildlife in Sepahijala Sanctuary, and immerse in Reang and Jamatia tribal traditions. Book now for an unforgettable journey!
