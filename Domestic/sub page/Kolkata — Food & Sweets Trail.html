Detailed Itinerary

Arrival in Kolkata → Hotel check-in → Evening food walk: Dekkers Lane puchka, Paramount lassi, Kusum rolls, Maharani kochuri, Fairlie Place telebhaja → Sweet stop at Balaram Mullick or KC Das → Overnight Kolkata.

Early morning Chinatown breakfast (pau-ruti, fish ball soup) → Bengali thali lunch at 6 Ballygunge Place or Bhajahari Manna → Visit famous sweet shops (mishti doi, sandesh, rosogolla) → Departure transfer with sweet memories!
