Tour Overview

Hidden gems of Silk Route – Sillery Gaon (Silent Valley) & Icchey Gaon (Wish Village). 360° Kanchenjunga view, pine forests, Ramitey View Point, Tinchuley, eco homestays & complete peace.

Detailed Itinerary

Pick-up from NJP/Bagdogra → Transfer to Sillery Gaon (Silent Valley) → Visit Tin Chuley View Point → Check-in homestay → Evening at leisure with Kanchenjunga view. Overnight Sillery Gaon.

Morning sunrise view → Visit Ramitey View Point (longest view of Teesta) → Transfer to Icchey Gaon (Wish Village) → Visit Damsang Fort & Sangchen Dorjee Monastery → Overnight Icchey Gaon.

Morning free for nature walk → After breakfast, departure transfer to NJP/Bagdogra. Trip ends with beautiful memories.
