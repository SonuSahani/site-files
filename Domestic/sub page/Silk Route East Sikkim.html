Tour Overview

Explore the historic Old Silk Route in East Sikkim – breathtaking high-altitude journey through Zuluk zig-zag roads, Lungthung, Gnathang Valley, Kupup Lake (Elephant Lake), Old Baba Mandir with stunning views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Detailed Itinerary

Pick-up from NJP/Bagdogra, proceed towards Zuluk. Enroute visit Lampokhari Lake (Aritar) & local sightseeing. Overnight at Zuluk homestay.

Early morning Thambi View Point for sunrise. Explore famous 99 zig-zag roads, Lungthung, Laxman View Point. Proceed to Gnathang Valley (Mini Switzerland). Overnight at Gnathang homestay.

Visit Kupup Lake (Elephant Lake), Yak Golf Course, Old Baba Mandir, Jelep La viewpoint. Enjoy snow (seasonal). Overnight at Gnathang/Zuluk homestay.

After breakfast, drive back to NJP/Bagdogra. Trip ends with beautiful memories of the Silk Route.
