Detailed Itinerary

Arrival in Kolkata → Hotel check-in (Park Street area) → Shopping at New Market & Gariahat → South City Mall → Evening at Park Street – rooftop bars, live music, clubs, street food → Overnight Kolkata.

Morning shopping at Quest Mall / Acropolis → Street shopping at Hatibagan or College Street → Lunch at famous eateries → Departure transfer. Return with bags full of memories!
