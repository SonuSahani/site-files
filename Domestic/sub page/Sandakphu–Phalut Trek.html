Detailed Itinerary

Arrive at NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport. Drive to Samanden (6-7 hours trek). Overnight stay in village homestay. Trek preparation and briefing.

Early morning trek to Sandakphu (11,941 ft). 5-6 hours steep climb through rhododendron forests. Camp at Sandakphu. Spectacular sunset views over Himalayan range.

Early morning sunrise view of Everest, Kanchenjunga, Makalu. Trek along the famous ridge to Phalut (7,215 ft). 4-5 hours scenic walk. Camp at Phalut.

Early morning visit to Phalut top for panoramic views. Day hike to nearby villages. Explore local culture and lifestyle. Overnight camping at Phalut.

Descend trek to Rammam (6-7 hours). Through dense forests and tribal villages. Overnight stay at Rammam forest rest house.

Trek to BhikeBh village (4-5 hours). Experience traditional village life. Cultural evening with local communities. Overnight in village homestay.

Morning drive to NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport (6-7 hours). Depart with unforgettable trekking memories.
