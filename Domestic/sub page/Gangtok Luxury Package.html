Detailed Itinerary

Arrive at Pakyong Airport. VIP transfer to your luxury hotel. Welcome drink and orientation. Evening leisure time at spa.

Morning visit to Rumtek Monastery, Do Drul Chorten, and Namgyal Institute. Afternoon cable car ride to Tashi Viewpoint. Evening cultural show with traditional dance performance.

Full day excursion to sacred Tsomgo Lake and optional visit to Nathula Pass (subject to permit availability). Yak ride and lunch at lakeside restaurant. Evening return to Gangtok.

Morning leisure time and spa treatment. Afternoon stroll along MG Marg for shopping and local cuisine. Evening farewell dinner with cultural program.

After breakfast, VIP transfer to Pakyong Airport for your journey back home with unforgettable luxury memories.
