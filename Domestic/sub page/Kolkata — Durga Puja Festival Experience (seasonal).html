Detailed Itinerary

Arrival in Kolkata → Visit Kumartuli idol-making studios → Experience Mahalaya → Evening preview of illuminated pandals → Welcome festive dinner.

Full-day award-winning pandals → Witness Sandhi Puja → Dhaki drums & dhunuchi naach → Street food trail → Late-night pandal visits.

Morning rituals → Red Road Carnival procession → Emotional idol immersion at Babughat → Grand farewell dinner.

Morning at leisure → Shopping for souvenirs → Departure transfer with lifetime memories.
