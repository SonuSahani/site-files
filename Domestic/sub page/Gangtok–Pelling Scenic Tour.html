Detailed Itinerary

Pick-up from NJP/Bagdogra → Transfer to Gangtok → Check-in hotel → Evening free at MG Marg. Overnight Gangtok.

Full day excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir → Return Gangtok → Visit Tashi View Point, Ganesh Tok, Enchey Monastery, Banjhakri Falls. Overnight Gangtok.

Transfer to Pelling via Ravangla → Visit Buddha Park & Temi Tea Garden → Reach Pelling → Evening free. Overnight Pelling.

Early morning Kanchenjunga view → Visit Pelling Skywalk, Chenrezig Statue, Pemayangtse Monastery, Rabdentse Ruins, Khecheopalri Lake, Kanchenjunga Falls. Overnight Pelling.

After breakfast, departure transfer to NJP/Bagdogra. Trip ends with beautiful memories.
