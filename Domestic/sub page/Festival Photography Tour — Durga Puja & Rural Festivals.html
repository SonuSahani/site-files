Detailed Itinerary

Arrival → Hotel check-in → Evening visit to top theme pandals with special photo passes → Night photography session → Overnight Kolkata.

Early morning Kumari Puja photography → Sandhi Puja at famous pandals → Dhunuchi Naach → Traditional Bengali lunch → More pandal hopping → Overnight Kolkata.

Day trip to rural Bengal → Capture village Durga Puja, tribal dances, fair, traditional games → Return to Kolkata for night pandals.
