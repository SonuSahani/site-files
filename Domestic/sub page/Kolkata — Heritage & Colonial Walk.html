Detailed Itinerary

Arrival in Kolkata → Hotel check-in → Start walking tour from Dalhousie Square (BBD Bagh) → Writers’ Building, GPO, St. John’s Church, Raj Bhavan, Old Currency Building, Old Mission Church → Evening in Old Chinatown (Tiretta Bazaar) → Overnight Kolkata.

Morning heritage walk: Maghen David Synagogue, Beth El Synagogue, Armenian Church, Mullick Ghat Flower Market → Visit Prinsep Ghat → Free time for photography/shopping → Departure transfer. Trip ends.
