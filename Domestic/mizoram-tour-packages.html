MIZORAM TOUR PACKAGES

Discover the land of the highlanders. From the scenic hills of Aizawl to the serene blue mountains of Champhai, experience Mizoram's pristine beauty and rich Mizo culture.

Trek to Phawngpui (Blue Mountain), explore Tamdil Lake, witness the Chapchar Kut festival, and discover the unique bamboo dance of the Mizo people.
